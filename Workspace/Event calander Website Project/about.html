A Little About Us

Our Mission

Welcome to Your Annoying Neighbor's Calendar, the one-stop destination for tracking all those neighborhood events you never asked to know about but somehow need to be aware of. We understand the struggle of living next to neighbors who love to throw impromptu backyard barbecues, host surprise garage sales, and organize block parties without proper notice. Our mission is simple: keep you informed (whether you want to be or not) about every single happening in your neighborhood.

What We Do

We've created a comprehensive calendar system that tracks everything from late-night basketball games to early morning yard work sessions. Our platform allows neighbors to submit events, share information about upcoming gatherings, and stay connected with their community. Whether it's a birthday party that goes until midnight, a garage sale that starts at 6 AM, or those wind chimes that seem to ring at the most inconvenient times, we've got it covered. We believe that knowledge is power, even when that knowledge involves knowing your neighbor is having another "surprise" party.

Join Our Community

Being part of Your Annoying Neighbor's Calendar means never being caught off guard again. You'll know exactly when to expect loud music, when to avoid parking in front of certain houses, and when to plan your own quiet activities. Our community of users helps each other stay informed and prepared for whatever neighborhood event comes next. So whether you're trying to plan around your neighbor's schedule or just want to stay in the loop, we're here to help you navigate the sometimes chaotic world of neighborhood living.

Our Services Include:

  • Event tracking and scheduling
  • Neighborhood notifications
  • Community calendar management
  • Noise complaint logging

How to Report an Event:

  1. Fill out our contact form
  2. Provide event details and time
  3. Submit your report
  4. We'll add it to the calendar
