About Us

So here's the deal - I had to make a website for a restaurant for my coding class, so naturally I created a restaurant just so I could make the website. It's that simple! We may have started as a class assignment, but we're serving up real vibes and imaginary delicious food that would totally be amazing if it were real!


Think of us as the restaurant that exists in the best timeline - where every burger is perfectly cooked, every pizza has the perfect cheese pull, and every taco is exactly what you need. We're here to feed your soul (and hopefully get a good grade). So come for the memes, stay for the... well, stay for the memes!
